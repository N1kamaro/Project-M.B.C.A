My steps to develop my first real rocket engine based on liquid propelent.

05/04 All the idea started as an simple engine, just an simple idea to make an real rocket engine just powerful enough to understand real rocket phisics.

05/09 I started doing some research and understanding better how rockets really works, the possibilities and with AI i finally had finished the combustion chamber in day 05/12, the appearence is not the better, but it works really nice and can handle extreme situations such as extreme heat and explosions.

05/14 After finishing the combustion chamber, i started making the Nozzle i took three days to complete it, i made it two separated parts because the nozzle is the part that is more affected by the heat that makes it pass through a huge chance to melt, so i made it to be the most efficient as possible and removable so i can just swap it if it melts.

05/17 After finishing the Nozzle i discovered some design problems, the "wall" were not enough to put an screw, so i increased the size of it, the vantage is that i also increased the heat resistance in the combustion chamber.
